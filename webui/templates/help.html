Help

You can find Guidelines, Instructions and General information in this section.

The Vocab.ox.ac.uk web store will be housed on the Bodleian Libraries Digital Asset Management System (DAMS). The DAMS provides a generic platform for the storage and preservation of digital objects within an RDF/Semantic Web framework.

    Vocabularies hosted at Oxford

    • Each vocabulary should have a designated individual assigned as the vocabulary ‘owner’ who will take responsibility for deposit, updating and queries about the vocabulary. The owner is required to provide a current email address so they can be contacted about queries referring to their vocabulary. Ownership can be transferred to a different individual by the owner if necessary.
    • Vocabularies are submitted in RDF and converted to HTML for display. Vocabularies can be downloaded in either RDF or HTML. Vocabularies are published as open linked data.
    • Suggestions for amendments to Oxford vocabularies can be sent to vocab.ox where they will be passed to the vocabulary owner.
    • The most recent version of a vocabulary held by vocab.ox will be displayed
    • A basic versioning mechanism is provided so that the history of updates to objects is recorded and acted upon if required
    • We reserve the right to refuse and/or remove inappropriate material.
    • If you only want to create one or two terms, and don't want to create your own full schema you might prefer to submit them to OpenVocab (openvocab.org), a community maintained site, where you can create and maintain terms.

    To submit a vocabulary to vocab.ox

    • Only members of the University of Oxford are eligible to submit vocabularies to vocab.ox
    • Go to vocab.ox.ac.uk and click on ‘Contribute’
    • Oxford vocabulary creators need their WebAuth SSO username and password to deposit their vocabulary.
    • If you are a new user, complete the registration details which should include your Oxford email address (ox.ac.uk).
    • If you are a returning user, log-in.
    • Fill in the details and upload your vocabulary in RDF. The RDF will be validated and any errors will be reported. You can fix the errors and re-submit.
    • A suggested schema of RDF for submitted vocabularies will be made available. If this style is followed, an HTML version of the vocabulary will be created for display. If not, the vocabulary will only be available in RDF. Vocabularies can be downloaded in either RDF or HTML.
    • Vocabularies are published as open linked data
    • Only vocabulary owners are permitted to amend their own vocabularies. Log-in, edit the raw RDF and save. The most recent version will be displayed on the website
    • Vocabularies will be hosted and maintained with the aim of perpetual display and access. Vocabularies cannot be deleted. If you can no longer maintain your vocabulary, or use of a vocabulary is deprecated, you could change the status of the vocabulary to reflect that. Vocabularies will only be removed from the site in consultation with the vocabulary owner.

    Referenced Vocabularies

    • Wherever possible, a local copy of external vocabularies will be ‘mirrored’ at Oxford to reduce the risk of them disappearing from web access.
    • Mirrored vocabularies will occasionally be synchronized with the original source. The version number of the mirrored version will be clearly displayed. Users can alert Bodleian Libraries staff to the need to update a version by contacting them using the vocab.ox contact details.
    • Members of the University can send suggestions for inclusion of externally hosted vocabularies that are in use at Oxford via the ‘contact us’ email
    • External vocabularies will only be mirrored in compliance with rights and permissions. If the data cannot be mirrored, a link to the source will be provided.
    • Suggestions for amendments and additions to external vocabularies should be directed to the owner at the original source.

    Rights and privacy

    • Vocabulary owners can assign a licence to their vocabularies. If no licence is included with deposited vocabularies, a Creative Commons Attribution – Sharealike licence will be attached
    • Details of people who register with the site in order to deposit vocabularies will only be used for that purpose and for management and maintenance of the site and will not be shared. Details of the site’s privacy policy will be displayed on the web site.

    Vocab.ox contains many types of vocabularies such as controlled vocabularies, ontologies, thesauri and classifications. An explanation of these terms can be found in the article "What are the differences between a vocabulary, a taxonomy, a thesaurus, an ontology, and a meta-model?" by Woody Pidcock and available at http://infogrid.org/wiki/Reference/PidcockArticle [accessed 30.11.10]

    Preservation and removal

    • Vocab.ox.ac.uk is built on the Bodleian Libraries’ archival DAMS. Vocabularies will be hosted and maintained with the aim of perpetual display and access.
    • Vocabularies will be removed from the site in consultation with the vocabulary owner.
    • We reserve the right to refuse and/or remove inappropriate material.

    How do I delete a vocabulary?

    Vocabularies cannot be deleted. If you can no longer maintain your vocabulary, or use of a vocabulary is deprecated, you could change the status of the vocabulary to reflect that.

    Who can use vocab.ox vocabularies?

    Vocabulary owners can assign a licence to their vocabularies. Otherwise vocabularies are made freely available under a CC Attribution-ShareAlike licence.
