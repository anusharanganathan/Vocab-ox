About the Project

Vocab.ox.ac.uk is hosted and maintained by SERS (Systems and e-Research Service), a department of The Bodleian Libraries. Project funding from PICT (PRAC ICT committee) is enabling the Bodleian Libraries to develop vocab.ox into a fully developed central service so that members of the University can deposit and disseminate controlled vocabularies produced at the University of Oxford. The new service will be launched in April 2011.

The Vocab.ox.ac.uk web store is housed on the Bodleian Libraries Digital Asset Management System (DAMS). The DAMS provides a generic platform for the storage and preservation of digital objects within an RDF/Semantic Web framework.
